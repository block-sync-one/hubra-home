## The technical side: how ra tokens work

When you deposit, you receive ra&#123;asset&#125; tokens (like raUSDC or raSOL). These are yield-bearing tokens that follow an exchange-rate model.

Your token balance stays the same. The value per token increases as yield accrues.

**Example:**
- You deposit 1,000 USDC
- You receive raUSDC based on the current exchange rate
- Over time, 1 raUSDC becomes worth more than 1 USDC
- When you withdraw, you get your principal plus all earned yield

This is the same model used by Compound&apos;s cTokens and Aave&apos;s aTokens. No claiming rewards. No staking. Just hold the token and its value grows.

## Safety controls

Our AI is not reckless. Every decision passes through multiple filters:

- **TVL threshold:** Only deposits into pools with $500k+ in assets
- **Dilution check:** If our deposit would significantly impact the APY, we skip it
- **Liquidity verification:** No pools with withdrawal restrictions
- **Fallback logic:** If no pool meets our criteria, funds stay idle rather than taking unnecessary risk

All transactions appear in your activity feed with explanations of what happened and why.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is my money locked?

No. You can withdraw anytime. There are no cooldown periods or lockups.

Copilot only moves funds to vetted protocols that pass our safety filters. All decisions are logged and visible in your activity feed. You can disable Copilot anytime and manage funds manually.

### How is this different from other yield aggregators?

Most aggregators show you options and let you choose. Hubra&apos;s AI actually executes. It monitors rates continuously and moves your funds automatically when better opportunities appear.

### What are ra tokens?

ra tokens (raUSDC, raSOL, etc.) represent your deposit plus earned yield. They use an exchange-rate model where your balance stays constant but the value per token increases over time.
