## What Are Hubra Vaults?

Hubra Vaults are yield-optimized pools that automatically allocate your capital to the highest-earning opportunities across Solana DeFi.

Behind the scenes, our AI rebalancer evaluates yields from Kamino, Drift, Jupiter, and other protocols every 2 minutes. When it finds a better opportunity that passes our safety filters, it moves your funds — automatically.

You don&apos;t compare protocols. You don&apos;t calculate slippage. You don&apos;t set alarms for yield changes. The vault does the work.

## How the AI Rebalancer Works

Every 2 minutes, Hubra&apos;s rebalancer runs a yield optimization pipeline:

1. **Fetches live yields** from all integrated protocols
2. **Filters for safety** — minimum TVL, established protocols only
3. **Calculates dilution** — won&apos;t dump into small pools that tank yields
4. **Executes migration** — moves withdrawable funds to the winner

If no pool passes the safety filters, funds stay put. No risky moves, no blind chasing.

The system also runs reactive rebalancing — when you deposit, it immediately puts your funds to work in the best available opportunity rather than waiting for the next cycle.
